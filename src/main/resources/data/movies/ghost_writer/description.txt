"Ghostwriters" are known in the publishing world as "literary ghosts." Their lives and creative struggles are unknown to anyone, but the results of their work become bestsellers.
One of them dies a mysterious death just before submitting a future bestseller, the memoirs of former British Prime Minister Adam Lang. To complete the politician's "work," the publishing house hires a professional ghostwriter.
