Quentin Tarantino recently reflected on his collaboration with Robert De Niro in the 1997 film "Jackie Brown," praising De Niro's transformative performance. Tarantino highlighted De Niro's commitment to his role, which showcased a different side of the actor's talent. The director shared insights into their working relationship and the creative process behind the film, emphasizing how De Niro's portrayal added depth and complexity to the character. This retrospective comes as "Jackie Brown" continues to be celebrated for its unique place in Tarantino's filmography.
